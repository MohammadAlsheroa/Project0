Manga are comics or graphic novels created in Japan or using the Japanese language
and conforming to a style developed in Japan in the late 19th century. They have a long
and complex prehistory in earlier Japanese art. The term manga is used in Japan to refer
to both comics and cartooning. Outside Japan, the word is used to refer to comics originally
published in Japan.
